Cristiano Ronaldo сareer

In July 2002, coach Laszlo Bölöni, impressed by Ronaldo's dribbling skills, invited him to take part in first-team pre-season sparring sessions. Sporting beat Rio Maior 5-0. After this match, Bölöni decided to move Ronaldo from the center of the field to the flank in order to better use his speed. On August 3, in a test match against Real Betis, Cristiano came on as a substitute and scored a goal, which helped his team win 3-2. At the same time, he played for the Portuguese national team at the European Under-17 Football Championship. On August 14, Cristiano made his debut in official matches for the club in the Champions League qualification against Internazionale.

Lionel Messi сareer

After the tournament in Japan, Barcelona C coach Pere Gratkos drew attention to Messi, he asked the director of the football academy to transfer Messi to his team. Soon Lionel was transferred to Barcelona B. Messi simultaneously played for both farm clubs, but in the 2003/04 season he played more for Barcelona C, which he helped to avoid relegation. On November 16, 2003, Messi made his debut for the main team of Barcelona in a friendly game against Porto. Head coach Frank Rijkaard released Lionel as a substitute towards the end of the game. Messi had two scoring chances, but his team lost 0-2. The debut of Lionel Messi in an official match took place on October 16, 2004, he came out eight minutes before the end of the match against Espanyol.

Kylian Mbappe сareer

In 2015 he made his debut in adult football. On December 2, in the match of the French championship against Cana, Fabio Coentrao replaced in the 88th minute. After the debut, he began to be regularly involved in games for the main team of Monaco. On December 10, he played in European competition for the first time - in the Europa League match with Tottenham, he scored an assist. In his first season, he played 14 matches in all competitions, scored one goal and gave two assists, while breaking several Thierry Henry records at once, becoming the youngest debutant in the history of the club and the youngest goal scorer.
